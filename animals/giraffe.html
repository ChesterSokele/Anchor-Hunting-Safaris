About the Giraffe

The world's tallest land animal, the giraffe is a uniquely specialised and unforgettable hunt. Their sheer height and unusual anatomy make shot placement a science of its own. A giraffe hunt is a bucket-list experience that yields an exceptional and one-of-a-kind trophy.

Hunting Tips

  • Shot placement is absolutely critical — the heart is positioned much lower in the chest than most animals.
  • A powerful caliber (.375 or larger) is required to penetrate the thick hide and reach vital organs.
  • Approach from behind cover and wait for the giraffe to come broadside before shooting.
  • Your professional hunter will walk you through the correct anatomy before you take the shot.
