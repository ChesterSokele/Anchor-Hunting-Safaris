About Anchor Hunting Safaris

Located in the heart of Namibia, Anchor Hunting Safaris spans 26,000 acres of pristine savanna bushveld, offering an exclusive hunting experience. Situated roughly 250 miles north-east of Windhoek and just 85 miles from the Botswana border, our privately owned game ranch is home to 18 huntable species.

Specializing in plains game hunting, we are committed to delivering a high-quality, ethical experience, guided by our team of professional hunters.

At Anchor Hunting Safaris, we offer more than just a hunt; we provide an immersive African experience, where clients can enjoy the beauty and challenge of the wild while contributing to conservation.
