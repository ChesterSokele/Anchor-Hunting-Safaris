About Anchor Hunting Safaris

Located in the heart of Namibia, Anchor Hunting Safaris spans 26,000 acres of pristine savanna bushveld, offering an exclusive hunting experience in one of Africa's most captivating landscapes. Situated roughly 250 miles north-east of Windhoek and just 85 miles from the Botswana border, our privately owned game ranch is home to 18 huntable species, providing a diverse range of hunting opportunities.

Specializing in plains game hunting, we are committed to delivering a high-quality, ethical experience, guided by our team of professional hunters. Our focus on sustainable practices ensures that each adventure supports the preservation of the local ecosystem and wildlife for future generations.

At Anchor Hunting Safaris, we offer more than just a hunt; we provide an immersive African experience, where clients can enjoy the beauty and challenge of the wild while contributing to the conservation of its unique species. Whether you're an experienced hunter or new to the sport, our expertly guided safaris promise an unforgettable adventure.
